Fil-C is a personal passion project by Filip Pizlo. Filip is the director of programmability and data model at Roblox. Before that, he was the senior director of language engineering at Epic Games and manager of the JavaScriptCore team at Apple. Most of the work on Fil-C happened as an approved outside activity while Filip was at Epic; hence you will see Epic copyrights in many Fil-C files. Filip has over 15 years of experience building high-performance implementations of high-level languages.
